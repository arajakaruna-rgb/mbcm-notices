Proxy Limits
Under Section 89D of the Owners Corporations and Other Acts Amendments Act 2021, one person may only hold a proxy for one lot owner in an OC with 20 or fewer lots.

You can submit your proxy digitally using the button below. Alternatively, complete and return the paper Owners Corporation Proxy Form to our office before the meeting. If you are appointing a Power of Attorney, please attach a copy.
